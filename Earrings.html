CHOOSE Sliver Gallery EARRINGS BASED ON YOUR FACE SHAPE

  1. Angular Face Shape: Opt for long dangles or geometric designs to elongate your appearance and add an enchanting allure.
  2. Oval Face Shape: Lucky you! Oval faces can rock a wide range of earring styles. Experiment with studs, hoops, and dangles of different shapes and lengths.
  3. Square Face Shape: Soften your angles that boast curved or rounded designs. Hoops, circular studs, or those with softer lines can be particularly flattering.
  4. Heart-Shaped Face: Balance your heart-shaped face with earrings that add width and volume at the jawline. Look for wide or triangular-shaped ones for an enchanting effect.
  5. Rectangular Face Shape:Offset the length of your face with earrings that add width and volume. Opt for wider hoop, clustered studs, or ones with wider, shorter dangles.
  6. Diamond Face Shape:Enhance your captivating angles with geometric or angular designs Triangle-shaped earrings, angular studs, or diamond-shaped drops can work wonders.
